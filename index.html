facts about German Shepherds

here are a few facts about german shepherds:

  • German Shepherds earned their name due to their talent for herding sheep
  • German Shepherds rank within the three most intelligent dog breeds
  • German Shepherds will explore everything with their noses
  • German shepherds are very protective of their loved ones
  • German Shepherds are known as a mouthy breed They tend to use their mouths as an appendage thanks to their herding heritage
